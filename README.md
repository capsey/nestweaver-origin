Inspired by Broodmother origin from [Origins++](https://modrinth.com/mod/origins-plus-plus) and [Reincarnation Origins: Broodmother](https://modrinth.com/mod/reincarnation-origins-broodmother).

To use this mod it's required to install [Origins (Fabric)](https://modrinth.com/mod/origins).

This mod modifies spider behavior so that they can be fed, leashed and have owners that they follow around and protect.

# Nestweaver

Powerful arachnid matriarch. Carry the nest within, and let your children walk beside you as shield and shadow.

* **Climbing** - You are able to climb up any kind of wall, not just ladders
* **Unwieldy** - The way your hands are formed provide no way of holding a shield upright
* **Carnivore** - You diet is restricted to meat, you can't eat vegetables
* **Fragility** - You have two hearts less health
* **Master of Webs** - You can freely more through and climb cobwebs, trap enemies and see anyone intruding your nest through vibrations in the web
* **Home** - You spawn in a mineshaft
* **Eyes of the Deep** - Your eyes are equipped to lurk in the shadows
* **Pest Control** - You take additional damage to fire
* **Poison Immunity** - You are immune to poison
* **Hatch** - Use ability to hatch two spiderlings that will follow and protect you. Look after them, they are your blood
* **Spider Kinship** - Spiders and Cave Spiders are not aggressive towards you, and you are able to feed them
* **Venomous Bite** - Hitting an enemy with an empty hand will inflict poison and restore some of your hunger
